Off Grid

An off-grid solar system pulls energy from the sun and its rays through solar panels, which most often are mounted to the roof of a home, cabin or RV. The energy the panels collect is then routed to a battery that stores the energy until you need it, when an inverter converts it into electricity.With the help of cutting-edge off-grid solar systems from RNR Power Controls, you can produce and store your own energy without relying on the conventional power grid.

Our strong and dependable off-grid options are perfect for anyone looking for total energy autonomy, emergency backup, or distant locations. We provide energy-efficient batteries, intelligent controllers, and high-performance solar panels that combine to deliver reliable power—even in the most difficult conditions. You may experience the independence of self-sufficient, renewable energy with RNR electricity Controls, which will lower your carbon footprint and guarantee that you have electricity whenever and wherever you need it.



On Grid

On-grid means your solar system is tied to your local utility’s GRID. This is what most residential homes will use because you are covered if your solar system under or over-produces in regard to your varying energy needs. All this means for you is that your utility system acts as your battery space. Our on-grid solar systems at RNR Power Controls are made to work smoothly with the current electrical infrastructure, offering dependable and reasonably priced renewable energy.

As pioneers in the solar industry, we create advanced inverters and high-efficiency photovoltaic panels that harness sunshine to generate electricity, minimizing your need on external power sources. With our on-grid solutions, you may feed surplus power back into the grid to receive credits through net metering, which lowers your energy expenses as well. You may benefit from continuous power and contribute to a sustainable future with RNR Power Controls, all supported by our dedication to quality and innovation.
